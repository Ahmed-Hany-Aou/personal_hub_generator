# Design Context

## Users
Independent designers, entrepreneurs, and creatives seeking a high-end digital identity kit. They need a professional "Personal Hub" that includes both a printable physical asset (Business Card) and a high-fidelity digital presence (PWA Landing Page). The 24/7 accessibility of a PWA is a core value proposition.

## Brand Personality
- **Elevated**: Feels premium and curated, not generic.
- **Fluid**: Interactions are smooth and tactile (drags, hovers, transitions).
- **Empowering**: Simple controls that produce complex, professional results.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: High-contrast, dark-mode-first aesthetic.
- **Palette**: Deep void backgrounds (`#060d14`) with tactical Cyan (`#22d3ee`) and Slate (`#7a8a9e`) secondary tones.
- **Visual Style**: Clean typography (Inter/Poppins), subtle glassmorphism, and neon-adjacent accents for a modern "Creative Studio" tech-forward look.
- **Mobile First**: All generated assets must be optimized for mobile viewports as the primary use case (scanning QR -> Landing Page).

## Design Principles
1. **The "Live" Experience**: Editor interactions (like the draggable toolbar) must feel responsive and low-latency.
2. **Subscription-Ready Quality**: The UI must justify a future premium/subscription model through elite aesthetics and functional stability.
3. **PWA Excellence**: The generated landing page must be a "Zero 404", fully responsive, PWA-standard mobile site.
